Pancakes

Ingredients needed for this recipe:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 4 tsp of baking Powder
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of milk
  • 4 tbsp of butter
  • Vegetable oil
  • Choice of topping: i.e. maple syrup

Steps:

  1. Whisk the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a large bowl
  2. In a different bowl, whisk the eggs and milk together until combined
  3. Pour the liquid mixture and the melted butter into the dry ingredients bowl
  4. Whisk mixture until fully combined. If the batter is too thick, add 1-2 tbsp more milk
  5. Heat a pan over medium heat and coat lightly with vegetable oil
  6. Place approximately 1/4 cup of the batter into the pan to make each pancake
  7. Cook each side until golden brown
  8. Wipe the pan with a paper towel between batches
  9. Serve with prepared toppings of choice
